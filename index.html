Nashville, Tennessee · (615) 586-5886 · AndrewoHerring@gmail.com

Adventurer/Teacher struggling with a coding addiction.

After discovering art school wasn't for me I joined the work force back in 2010 to get by. While I thrived and moved up wherever I was, a desire to see and be more grew quickly after working one too many retail jobs. In 2015 I bought a one way plane ticket to (at the time) The Czech Republic (now Czechia) to live and work in its capital, Prague.

Once I arrived I attended TEFL Worldwide Prague, to become certified in teaching English as a second language. Once I graduated I was hired by The Caledonian School, one of Prague's largest ESL schools. After working and living there for about a year and half the desire to achieve more started to take root again. I had friends who had been telling me about their careers and success in software development and became intrigued.

After some research and saving up I decided to attend Nashville Software School full time. Along with the skills I've learned, I've discovered a new passion. Creative problem solving with endless room for growth mixed with the feeling of making something with your hands meant that for me, this will be a lifelong pursuit.
